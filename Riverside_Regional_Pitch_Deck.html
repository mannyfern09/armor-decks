Behavioral Health
Mental Health Services
A dedicated behavioral health team integrated with the medical clinic — built around early identification, active monitoring, and continuity into the community on release.
Suicide Prevention
Screen · Monitor · Respond
"Every patient screened. Every event reviewed. Every gap closed."
  • Suicide risk screen at intake — every patient, every booking
  • Validated screening tools by trained staff
  • Constant or close observation protocols based on acuity
  • Suicide-resistant housing & equipment review
  • Post-event debrief & mortality review with corrective action
  • Annual all-staff suicide prevention training
Mental Health Assessments
Standardized · Documented · Repeatable
"From intake screen to comprehensive evaluation in 14 days or less."
  • Receiving screening at intake — every patient
  • Comprehensive MH evaluation within 14 days when indicated
  • Standardized tools: PHQ-9, GAD-7, AUDIT, COWS
  • Diagnosis & care plan documented in EHR
  • Provider-level evaluation by licensed clinicians
  • Re-evaluation triggered by acute events or self-referral
Medication Monitoring
Continuity · Compliance · Safety
"No psychotropic prescription falls through the cracks — at intake, in care, or at release."
  • Psychotropic medication continuity from community at intake
  • Electronic MAR (eMAR) with directly observed therapy
  • Provider follow-up — minimum every 90 days
  • Side-effect monitoring & AIMS assessment as indicated
  • Lab monitoring per drug-specific protocols (lithium, clozapine)
  • Medication reconciliation at every transition of care
Telepsychiatry
Access · Speed · Coverage
"Board-certified psychiatry on demand — no transports, no delays."
  • Board-certified psychiatrists available via secure video
  • Same-week appointment availability for urgent referrals
  • Integrated documentation directly into the EHR
  • Backup & on-call coverage nights, weekends, holidays
  • Expands access without sacrificing clinical quality
  • Ideal complement to on-site BH staff for medication management

Transition Plan
A Proven, Phased Approach
Our transition methodology is the same playbook we have executed across dozens of facility start-ups. This transition will be delivered in 6 weeks, with a target go-live of July 1, 2026 — explicit owners, milestones, and certified completion gates at every stage. Nothing moves to the next phase until the prior gate is clean.
PHASE 1
Mobilize
Contract Award → Day 10
  • Executive kickoff with County leadership
  • Site walkthroughs & inventory
  • Stand up the transition team
  • Review contract, staffing matrix, reporting
PHASE 2
Build
Day 11 → Day 35
  • Recruit & credential HSA, MD, DON
  • Onboard transitioning staff
  • Stand up vendors (pharmacy, lab, radiology)
  • IT infrastructure & EHR training
PHASE 3
Go-Live
Day 36 → July 1
  • Final dry runs & staffing schedule posted
  • Pharmaceuticals, supplies, equipment in place
  • Day 1 command center on-site
  • Joint rounds with clinical leadership
PHASE 4
Stabilize
Post Go-Live → Day 90
  • KPI scorecard reporting cadence
  • First operations audit
  • MAC / CQI / P&T committees seated
  • Continuous improvement loop engaged
OUR COMMITMENT: Zero disruption to patient care on Day 1. No lapses in medications, offsite appointments, or chronic care.

Workforce Plan
How We Recruit & Retain
Retention is treated as an ongoing operational discipline — not a one-time event at contract start. We invest in proven programs, market-data discipline, and concierge-level support to make Armor a place where clinicians want to stay.
  • Economic Research Institute (ERI) — market-based compensation, benchmarked not guessed; reviewed and adjusted regularly
  • Fidelity Nurse Loan Payment — recurring student loan repayment program that materially improves nurse retention
  • White Glove Treatment — concierge onboarding with dedicated recruiter, expedited credentialing, and a structured 90-day check-in cadence
  • Tiered Nursing Rates — differentiated pay structure that rewards experience and certifications, creating a clear path forward without leaving the role
BOTTOM LINE: Riverside Regional inherits this retention program from Day 1 — built to keep your clinicians in place.
